About Me

As a 30-year-old aspiring Fullstack Developer, I bring a unique blend of technical expertise and a determination to expand my skillset. My background as an EPC Technician has instilled in me a keen eye for detail and a systematic approach to problem-solving, qualities that I believe will be invaluable in my transition to web development. I am continuously immersing myself in the latest web technologies, frameworks, and design principles, striving to stay ahead in this dynamic field. I firmly believe that learning is a lifelong journey, and I am committed to honing my craft to deliver seamless and user-centric web solutions.

My portfolio showcases my projects and progress as I take steps towards achieving my dream of becoming a proficient Fullstack Developer. From frontend design to backend logic, I relish the process of bringing ideas to life and creating digital products that make a positive impact on users. When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring the picturesque landscapes of Austria or enjoying some downtime with a cup of coffee and a good book. Nature and creativity play an essential role in keeping me inspired and motivated on my web development journey. Thank you for visiting my portfolio website, and I hope my projects reflect my passion for web development and my dedication to becoming a skilled Fullstack Developer.
